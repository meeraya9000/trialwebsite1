Italian almond cookies, also known as amaretti cookies, are delightful treats that originate from Italy. These cookies are renowned for their delicate and chewy texture, with a slight crispness on the outside. The rich almond flavor takes center stage, providing a delightful nutty taste that lingers with every bite. The cookies are often light and airy, making them an ideal accompaniment to a cup of coffee or tea. They are commonly enjoyed as a sweet treat after a meal or as a delightful snack any time of day. Italian almond cookies come in various shapes and sizes, with the traditional version typically shaped into small, round morsels. Some variations include a dusting of powdered sugar or a light glaze, enhancing their visual appeal while adding a touch of sweetness. These cookies are beloved for their simplicity and timeless appeal. While they are a classic Italian treat, their popularity has spread worldwide, captivating the taste buds of cookie lovers everywhere. Whether enjoyed as a special indulgence during holidays or as a year-round delicacy, Italian almond cookies offer a delightful blend of flavors and textures that are sure to please anyone with a penchant for almond-infused sweets. Their charm lies in their uncomplicated yet delectable nature, making them an irresistible addition to any dessert table or cookie jar.

Ingredients: almonds, almond extract, sugar, egg whites and powdered sugar.
