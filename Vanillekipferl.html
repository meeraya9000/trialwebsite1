Vanillekipferl

Vanillekipferl is a delightful Austrian cookie known for its delicate crescent shape and rich vanilla flavor. These delectable treats are made with a simple but heavenly combination of high-quality ingredients, resulting in a tender and melt-in-your-mouth texture. The cookies are baked to a light golden hue and then coated in a generous dusting of powdered sugar, adding a touch of sweetness and creating an enticing snowy-white appearance. With their delicate texture and captivating aroma, Vanillekipferl cookies offer a delightful experience that evokes warmth, nostalgia, and pure indulgence. Whether enjoyed during the holiday season or any time of the year, these crescent-shaped delights are sure to enchant cookie enthusiasts with their irresistible charm.

Ingredients: Flour, Ground almonds, Butter, Powdered sugar, Vanilla extract
